REAL people with REAL remarks.

Walao-eh! serves as an outlet for people living in Kuala Lumpur to express their heartfelt feelings and concerns they face everyday. Tuan-tuan dan puan-puan, aunties and uncles, we definitely heard you!

Listen to their unfiltered emotions expressed through our beautiful "rojak" language:

Chan Boon Huat, 55 years old
"Young man nowadays ah, I tell you ah, all VERY lazy! We work so hard back then to build this country up, but now you guys don't want to work? How can like this la aiya?"
Atikah binti Abdul Razak, 36 years old
"I don't understand why so many people in this country like to complain this complain that. You not happy, go back to where you came from! Bersyukur sikit la! Malaysia negara terbest!"
Udhayan Nagaratnam, 42 years old
"Matcha! You guys always think we indians only know how to drink and fight. Come on la dei without us where got mamak for you guys to lepak?"
